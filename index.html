About the salon

A friendly Motueka salon that treats your hair like it matters

K & S Hair is a small, welcoming salon in the heart of Port Motueka, run by Katie & Shayna. We're the kind of place where you're greeted by name, offered a cuppa, and given the time to talk through what you actually want before a single snip.

Whether it's a maintenance trim, a big colour change or getting ready for a special day, we keep things relaxed, honest and easy — and we'll only ever suggest what genuinely suits you.
